About me

I am a software development student living in Gothenburg, with a background in culture and gender studies. Beyond coding, I have a profound love for cats, reading, indulging in afternoon naps, and eating anything involving potatoes.

Experience

During my career, I have been committed to promoting social inclusion and advancing equality. I've served as a project leader, creating inclusive activities for children with disabilities, and worked with unaccompanied refugee girls in residential care homes. I've been a supervisor in an integration project initiated by the City of Gothenburg and worked as a youth counselor in a recreational center.

Academically, I've explored a range of disciplines, including gender and cultural sciences, art history, and ceramics, as well as completing extensive coursework in both French and English. Now, stepping into the field of software development, I am excited to make use of my diverse background to innovate, collaborate, and contribute to the ever-evolving landscape of technology.
